About

A passionate Multimedia Designer, Tech Entreprenuer & Artist born in Zimbabwe, made in Africa.

"The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams." - Eleanor Roosevelt

This quote resonates with my upbringing and the values instilled in me. My parents encouraged my siblings and I to explore, have an open mind, and keep learning. They taught us the importance of respecting others and their values, and to let go of anything that didn't serve us well.

These lessons have played a significant role in shaping who I am today, from my journey's highs and lows to the joy I find in curating authentic experiences and sharing them with others. Through art and design, I've been able to communicate ideas and concepts that can be scaled, while also hoping to acquire tech skills and contribute to its advancement.

I'm hopeful that we can harness our collective potential to address some of the problems plaguing our society today.
