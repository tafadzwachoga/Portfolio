About


I am a multimedia designer with a proven track record in crafting compelling and innovative digital and print designs. My expertise extends to visual strategy, layout development, branding, print media, and advertising. Throughout my career, I have consistently achieved success in collaborative work environments.

Recently, I held the position of Creative Consultant at Design Lotus Boutique Studio, where I am also a business partner. Our primary focus revolves around tailoring unique visual identities for both new and established micro-brands. My responsibilities encompass UX/UI design, website development, digital content creation, and graphic design.

One year ago, I embarked on an enriching journey in the digital marketing field as a designer with TMI Collective, based in the vibrant city of Cape Town. My role was centered on crafting impactful marketing collateral, dynamic motion graphics for various platforms, including our social media channels, graphic design, as well as UX/UI and front-end website design. It was an experience that fueled my passion for creative innovation.
